AirBnB Learning Project

A project was recently completed by me that involved analyzing AirBnB data in Seattle during 2016 using Tableau. This project provided me with a more comprehensive understanding of AirBnB's pricing and revenue trends, and I was able to showcase my data processing and analysis skills using Tableau.

The project was split into three core areas: average price per bedroom, price per zip code, and annual revenue. I utilized AirBnB data from Seattle in 2016 to generate clear and understandable visualizations, which were guided by the tutorials of AlexTheAnalyst on YouTube.

Firstly, I calculated the average price per bedroom and created a bar chart to show how the average price varied depending on the number of bedrooms available. By visualizing this data, I was able to provide insights into the price range that can be expected based on the number of bedrooms available.

Next, I used a map and a bar chart format to illustrate the price per zip code. This method enabled me to demonstrate how prices differed across various areas in Seattle, resulting in a better comprehension of how Airbnb prices varied based on location.

Lastly, I created a yearly income visualization to understand income trends during 2016. This visualization helped me comprehend how AirBnB revenue in Seattle changed during the course of the year, and I was able to present this information in an informative and easy-to-understand format.

Overall, the project allowed me to improve my data processing and analysis skills while also gaining a deeper understanding of AirBnB pricing and revenue trends in Seattle during 2016. The tutorials by AlexTheAnalyst were incredibly helpful in guiding me through the process, and I am grateful for the knowledge and skills I gained through this project.

If you would like to see the full project, please follow this link: https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/mohamad.maulana.firdaus.ramadhan/viz/AirBnBLearningProject/Dashboard1
