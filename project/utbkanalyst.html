UTBK Score Analytics

The analysis conducted by the Student Association's Database Division on the distribution of UTBK scores for admission to STEI-K provides valuable insights into the performance of prospective students. The data reveals that the distribution of scores varies significantly, with an average UTBK score of 688.002 for admitted students. This indicates a relatively high level of difficulty for the exam, demonstrating that students admitted to STEI-K possess strong academic abilities and can handle challenging academic tasks.

Furthermore, the analysis found that the average academic report score for the 2022 class was 86.43. This suggests that students admitted to STEI-K have a very good academic track record and a proven ability to excel in their previous level of education. Such a strong academic background is a valuable asset in pursuing a career in the technology industry.

Overall, the insights gained from the analysis provide a better understanding of the academic capabilities of prospective students applying to STEI-K. These findings can assist in the development of admission criteria and policies that align with the goals and objectives of the institution, while also ensuring that the incoming class possesses the skills and abilities necessary to succeed in the technology industry.

For a complete statistical analysis of the UTBK and academic report scores for the 2022 class at STEI-K, interested parties can check out the results at bit.ly/StatUTBK_STEIK22. The analysis provides detailed insights into the distribution of scores and identifies patterns and trends in the data. It can be a valuable resource for anyone interested in understanding the academic performance of prospective students at STEI-K
